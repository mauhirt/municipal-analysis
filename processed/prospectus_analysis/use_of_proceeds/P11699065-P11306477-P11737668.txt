 Process for Evaluation and Selection 
  The Department maintains and updates at least an nually a long-range Capital Improvement Program 
(the “CIP”). The Department’s long-range CIP process, design standards, permitting and 2010 Consent Decree 
requirements guide the process for project evaluation and selection as does the Department’s objective of providing 
safe and reliable facilities to process the wastewater discharge of all sewer customers throughout Oahu.  The classes 
of CIP projects include system expansion, system  upgrade, and replacement/rehabilitation. See “CAPITAL 
IMPROVEMENT PROGRAM” below for additional informat ion regarding the CIP and capital expenditures and 
projects for the Wastewater System. 
 Management of Proceeds 
  Proceeds from the Series 2023 Bonds will be used  to finance the costs of certain additions and 
improvements to the Wastewater System. Net of certain transaction costs, the proceeds of the Series 2023 Bonds will 
be tracked and deposited into one or more separate accounts and will be used to finance eligible projects. 
 Reporting 
  The Department intends to produce annual update reports on the impact of the Series 2023 Bonds 
on various green projects until the proceeds have been fully allocated. 
 Not Suitable Investment for all Investors Seeking Exposure to Green Assets 
  The purpose of designating the Series 2023 Bonds as “Green Bonds” is to allow the owners of the 
Series 2023 Bonds to invest directly in bonds which finance environmentally beneficial projects. There is currently 
no clearly-defined definition (legal, regulatory or otherwise) of, nor market consensus as to what constitutes, a “green,” 
“environmental,” “sustainable” or equiva lently-labeled project, or as to what precise attributes are required for a 
particular project to be defined as “green,” “environmental,” “sustainable” or such other equivalent label, and nor can 
any assurance be given that such a clear definition or co nsensus will develop over time. Accordingly, although the 
Department anticipates that financed projects will be ali gned with the Green Bond Principles, no assurance can be 
given to investors that the financed projects will meet a ny or all investor expectations regarding such “green,” 
“environmental,” “sustainable” or other equivalently-l abeled performan ce objectives, or that any adverse 
environmental, sustainable and/or other impacts will not occur during the operation and maintenance of such projects. 
  The term “Green Bonds” is neither defined in nor related to provisions in the First Bond Resolution. 
The term “Green Bonds” is solely for identification purpose and is not intended to provide or imply that the owners 
of the Series 2023 Bonds are entitled to any security ot her than that described under the heading “SECURITY FOR 
THE BONDS” below.

Optional Redemption  
 
The Series 2023 Bonds maturing on or after July 1, 2034 are subject to redemption prior to the stated 
maturity thereof at the option of the City and County on or after July 1, 2033, in whole or in part at any time, from 
any maturities selected by the City and County (in its sole discretion), at a redemption price equal to 100% of the 
principal amount of the Series 2023 Bonds or portions thereof to be redeemed plus accrued interest to the date of 
redemption.   
Selection for Redemption  
 
If less than all of the Series 2023 Bonds of a series are called for redemption, the City and County 
will designate the maturities from which the Series 2023 Bonds of such series are to be redeemed.  For so long as the 
Series 2023 Bonds are registered in book-entry form and DTC or a successor securities depository is the sole registered 
owner of such Series 2023 Bonds, if fewer than all of the Series 2023 Bonds of the same maturity and bearing the 
same interest rate are to be redeemed, the particular Seri es 2023 Bonds to be redeemed shall be selected by lot in 
accordance with DTC procedures; provided that any such redemption must be performed such that all Bonds 
remaining outstanding will be in authorized denominations. See Appendix C, “Book-Entry System.”

Notice of Redemption 
Notice of redemption of any Series 2023 Bond is required to be mailed not less than 30 days prior 
to the redemption date to the holder of such Series 2023 Bond at its address as it appears on the registry books kept 
pursuant to the provisions of the First Bond Resolution.  Notice of redemption of Series 2023 Bonds will be given by 
the Paying Agent. 
Each notice of redemption is required to state: (i) the title of the Series 2023 Bonds to be redeemed, 
the subseries designation thereof (if any), the redemption date, the place or places of redemption and the redemption 
price payable upon such redemption; (ii) if less than all the Series 2023 Bonds are to be redeemed, the distinctive 
number of the Series 2023 Bonds to be redeemed, (iii) that th e interest on the Series 2023 Bonds, or on the principal 
amount thereof to be redeemed, designated for redemption in such notice shall cease to accrue from and after such 
redemption date; and (iv) that on such date there will become due and payable on each such Series 2023 Bond the 
principal amount thereof to be redeemed at the then app licable redemption price and th e interest accrued on such 
principal amount to the redemption date.  Each notice of redemption mailed to the holder of a Series 2023 Bond to be 
redeemed must, if less than the entire principal sum thereof is to be redeemed, also state the principal amount thereof 
to be redeemed and, if less than the entire principal sum of a Series 2023 Bond all of the principal of which matures 
on the same day is called for redemption, that such Series 2023 Bond must be surrendered to the Paying Agent in 
exchange for the payment of the principal amount thereof to be redeemed and the issuance of a new Series 2023 Bond 
or Series 2023 Bonds equaling in principal amount that portion of the principal sum not to be redeemed of the Series 
2023 Bond to be surrendered. 
Any notice of optional redemption may state that such redemption may be conditional upon the 
receipt by the Paying Agent on the date fixed for redemption of moneys sufficient to pay in full the redemption price 
of the Series 2023 Bonds proposed to be redeemed.  If the notice contains such condition, and moneys sufficient to 
pay in full the redemption price of the Se ries 2023 Bonds proposed to be redeemed shall not be received on or prior 
to the date fixed for redemption, such notice of redemption shall be null and void and of no force and effect, the City 
and County shall not redeem or be obligated to redeem any Series 2023 Bonds, and the Paying Agent at the City and 
County’s direction shall give notice, in the same manner as notice of redemption is given, that moneys sufficient to 
pay in full the redemption price of the Se ries 2023 Bonds proposed to be red eemed were not received on or prior to 
the date fixed for redemption and such redemption did not occur.  In the event of the failure to redeem, all Series 2023 
Bonds surrendered for redemption shall be promptly returned to the holder or holders by the Paying Agent. 
For so long as a book-entry only system is in effect with respect to the Series 2023 Bonds, notice of 
redemption, or notice of rescission of any conditional notice of redemption, of Series 2023 Bonds to be redeemed is
