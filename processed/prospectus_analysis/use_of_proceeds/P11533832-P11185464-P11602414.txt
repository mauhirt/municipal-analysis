PLAN OF REFUNDING 
The Authority is issuing the Bonds to provide moneys (together with other available funds of the 
Authority) necessary to refund its Revenue Bonds (Solar Renewable Energy Program) Issue of 2012A (the 
“Refunded Obligations”) in whole.  On the date of issuance of the Bonds, a portion of the proceeds of the 
Bonds and other available funds of the Authority will be transferred, pursuant to an escrow agreement for 
the Refunded Obligations (the “Escrow Agreement”), to U.S. Bank National Association (the “Escrow 
Bank”).  Such moneys shall be applied by the Escrow Bank to the payment and redemption of the Refunded 
Obligations on November 1, 2021. 
The amounts held by the Escrow Bank under the Escrow Agreement are pledged solely to the 
redemption of the outstanding Refunded Obligations.  The moneys deposited with the Escrow Bank under 
the Escrow Agreement will not be available for the payments of principal of and interest on the Bonds. 
Sufficiency of the deposits to the Escrow Fund for the payment and redemption of the Refunded 
Obligations will be verified by  Robert Thomas CPA, LLC , Minneapolis, Minnesota (the “Verification 
Agent”).  Assuming the accuracy of such computations, as a result of the deposit and application of funds 
as provided in the Escrow Agreement, the Refunded Obligations will be defeased pursuant to the provisions 
of the trust agreement under which such Refunded Obligations was issued as of the date of issuance of the 
Bonds.
