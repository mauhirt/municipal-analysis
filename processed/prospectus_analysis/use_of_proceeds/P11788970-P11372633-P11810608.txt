K. Moral Obligation Pledge
The 
City has entered into moral obligations with the following WEDA borrowings: Revenue Refunding Bonds Series 2012 
(Mandalay Gardens Urban Renewal), Revenue Refunding Bods Series 2022 (South Sheridan), and Revenue Loan Series 2012 
(North Huron) with an aggregate balance of $33,009,930 outstanding as of December 31, 2023. A moral obligation is a pledge by 
the City Manager to request of Council that the City replenish the Reserve Fund associated with this debt to the minimum required 
per the bond indenture or loan document should it become necessary. Council will consider, but is not obligated to fulfill this 
request.
