PLAN OF FINANCING 
The net proceeds of the 2021 Series A Bonds and other funds available under the Resolution are expected to 
be applied (i) to advance refund the Advance Refunded Bonds and (ii) to pay costs of issuance of the 2021 Series A 
Bonds. Upon delivery of the 2021 Series A Bonds, a portion of the proceeds of the 2021 Series A Bonds will be deposited 
into the Refunding Escrow Fund established by an Escrow Agreement between the Commission and U.S. Bank National 
Association, as Escrow Agent. The Refunding Escrow Fund w ill be invested in United States government or agency 
obligations maturing in amounts and bearing interest at rates sufficient, without further reinvestment, to pay, when due, 
interest on, and upon redemption, the outstanding principal of and redemption premium, if any, on the Advance 
Refunded Bonds.  The Refunding Escrow Fund, including the in terest earnings on the obligations therein, is pledged 
solely for the benefit of the holders of the Advance Refunded Bonds and is not available to pay the principal of and 
interest on the 2021 Bonds. 
The net proceeds of the 2021 Series B Bonds and other funds available under the Resolution shall be applied 
(i) to retire outstanding commercial paper issued by the Commission and thereby permanently finance the capital 
improvements originally financed by such commercial paper and (ii) to pay costs of issuance of the 2021 Series B 
Bonds.
