the purposes thereof. Moneys held in the Subordinated Debt Subaccounts shall not be invested except as 
otherwise permitted in the Resolution or in th e Bond Resolution for amounts on deposit in the 
Construction and Acquisition Fund.

impair any such right or remedy or  shall be construed to be a waiver of any such Event of Default or 
acquiescence therein; and every such right and remedy may be exercised from time to time and as often 
as may be deemed expedient.
