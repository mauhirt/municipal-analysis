for the foregoing purposes, are pledged solely for the benefit of the holders of the Senior Refunded Bonds and the Junior Refunded 
Bonds, respectively.  The Escrow Securities will be purchased from the Treasury Department of the United  States of America or 
in the open market, in either case at interest rates and prices which will cause the yield thereon, computed in accordance with the 
provisions of Section  148 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended (the “Code”), not to exceed the applicable yield 
permitted by such provisions.  See “VERIFICATION.” 
THE SERIES 2025 BONDS 
General 
The Senior Series 2025 Bonds and the Junior Series 2025 Bonds are secured by and issued pursuant to separate 
resolutions, issuance certificates and other proceedings.  The summary set forth below is presented in a consolidated manner for 
convenience. 
The Series 2025 Bonds will be dated as of the date of their issuance and will bear interest at the rates and mature 
on the dates and in the principal amounts set forth on the inside cover page s of this Official Statement.  The Series 2025 Bonds 
will be issued in minimum denominations of $5,000 principal amount.  Interest will accrue on the Series 2025 Bonds from the date 
of their issuance and will be due and payable semiannually on January 1 and July 1 of each year, commencing January 1, 2026 . 
U.S. Bank Trust Company, National Association (successor in interest to U.S. Bank National Association) will serve as the initial 
Paying Agent for the Series 2025 Bonds.

its successors, if any, in such capacity, the “Securities Depository”).  So long as the Securities Depository or its nominee is the

Optional Redemption  
 
The Senior Series 2025 Bonds maturing on or after July 1, 2036 are subject to redemption prior to the stated 
maturity thereof at the option of the City and County on or after July 1, 20 35, in whole or in part at any time, from any maturities 
selected by the City and County (in its sole discretion), at a redemption price equal to 100% of the principal amount of the Senior 
Series 2025 Bonds or portions thereof to be redeemed plus accrued interest to the date of redemption. 
The Junior Series 2025 Bonds are not subject to optional redemption prior to maturity.

Notice of Redemption 
Notice of redemption of any Senior Series 2025 Bond is required to be mailed not less than 30 days prior to the 
redemption date to the holder of such Senior Series 2025 Bond at its address as it appears on the registry books kept pursuant to 
the provisions of the Bond Resolutions.  Notice of redemption of Senior Series 2025 Bonds will be given by the Paying Agent.  
Each notice of redemption is required to state: (i) the title of the Senior Series 2025 Bonds to be redeemed, the 
subseries designation thereof (if any), the redemption date, the place or places of redemption and the redemption price payable 
upon such redemption; (ii) if less than all the Senior Series 2025 Bonds are to be redeemed, the distinctive number of the Series 
2025 Bonds  to be redeemed, (iii) that the interest on the Senior Series 2025 Bonds, or on the principal amount thereof to be 
redeemed, designated for redemption in such notice shall cease
