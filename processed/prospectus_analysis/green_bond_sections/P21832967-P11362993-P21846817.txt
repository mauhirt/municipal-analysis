Other Obligations

Ohio Environmental Protection Agency (“OEPA”)  and the Ohio Water Development Authority 
(“OWDA”).  The loans from the WPCLF Fund (“WP CLF Loans”) are payable by the Division from the 
Subordinate Debt Service Fund and thereby are subordinate to the debt service payable on all Bonds issued 
pursuant to the Trust Agreement.
