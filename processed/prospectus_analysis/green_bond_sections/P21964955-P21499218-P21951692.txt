 Process for Evaluation and Selection 
  The Department maintains and updates at least annually a long-range Capital Improvement Program (the “CIP”). 
The Department’s long-range CIP process, design standards, permitting and 2010 Consent Decree requirements guide the process 
for project evaluation and selection as does the Department’s objective of providing safe and reliable facilities to process the 
wastewater discharge of all sewer customers throughout Oahu.  The classes of CIP projects include system expansion, system 
upgrade, and replacement/rehabilitation. See “CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROGRAM” below for additional information 
regarding the CIP and capital expenditures and projects for the Wastewater System.

 Reporting 
  The Department intends to produce annual update reports on the impact of the Series 2025 Bonds on various 
green projects until the proceeds have been fully allocated. 
 Not Suitable Investment for all Investors Seeking Exposure to Green Assets 
  The purpose of designating the Series 2025 Bonds as “Green Bonds” is to allow the owners of the Series 2025 
Bonds to invest directly in bonds which finance environmentally beneficial projects. There is currently no clearly-defined definition 
(legal, regulatory or otherwise) of, nor market consensus as to what constitutes, a “green,” “environmental,” “sustainable” o r 
equivalently-labeled project, or as to what precise attributes are required for a particular project to be defined as “green,” 
“environmental,” “sustainable” or such other equivalent label, and nor can any assurance be given that such a clear definition or 
consensus will develop over time. Accordingly, although the Department anticipates that financed projects will be aligned with the 
Green Bond Principles, no assurance can be given to investors that the financed projects will meet any or all investor expectations 
regarding such “green,” “environmental,” “sustainable” or other equivalently-labeled performance objectives, or that any adverse 
environmental, sustainable and/or other impacts will not occur during the operation and maintenance of such projects. 
  The term “Green Bonds” is neither defined in nor related to provis ions in the First Bond Resolution . The term 
“Green Bonds” is solely for identification purpose and is not intended to provide or imply that the owners of the Series 2025 Bonds 
are entitled to any security other than that described under the heading “SECURITY FOR THE BONDS” below.
