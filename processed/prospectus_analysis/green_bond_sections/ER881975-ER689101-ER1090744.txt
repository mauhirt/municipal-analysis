GREEN BONDS DESIGNATION 
The 2015 Series A Bonds are being designated by the SFPUC as “Green Bonds.” The purpose of 
designating the offered bonds as “Green Bonds” is to a llow investors to invest directly in bonds which finance 
environmentally beneficial projects (“Green Projects”).  For purposes of such determination, the SFPUC considers 
the following to be Green Projects:  (i) Hetch Hetchy Project hydroelectric generation facilities, (ii) other renewable 
energy projects, such as biomass and biowaste, solar an d wind, and (iii) energy conservation projects, such as 
energy-efficient streetlights.  The reconstruction or replacement projects expected to be funded by the 2015 Series A 
Bonds are all generation components of the hydroelectric facilities of the Hetch Hetchy  Project, which produces 
greenhouse gas (“GHG”) free electricity. See PLAN OF FINANCE and POWER ENTERPRISE CAPITAL 
PROGRAM.  The 2015 Series A Bonds will not constitute “ex empt facility bonds” issued to finance “qualified 
green building and sustainable design projects” within the meaning of Section 142(1) of the Code.  The SFPUC 
expects to post information respecting the expenditure of the proceeds of the 2015 Series A Bonds on its website. 
The 2015 Series B Bonds are not being designated as “Green Bonds”.  The Indenture does not restrict the 
use of proceeds of future issuances of Bonds to the financing of Green Projects and in the future the SFPUC may 
issue Bonds which are not designated as Green Bonds.   
The repayment obligations of the Power Enterprise with respect to the 2015 Series AB Bonds are not 
conditioned on the completion of any particular project or the satisfaction of any certification relating to the status of 
the 2015 Series A Bonds as Green Bonds.  See SECURITY FOR THE BONDS.

THE 2015 SERIES AB BONDS 
General 
The 2015 Series AB Bonds will be dated as of their date of delivery and will accrue interest from the date 
of delivery at the rates per annum set forth on the inside cover page of this Official Statement.  Interest on the 2015 
Series AB Bonds is payable on May 1 and November 1 of each year, beginning November 1, 2015.  Interest on the 
2015 Series AB Bonds will be calculated on the basis of a 360-day year composed of twelve 30-day months. 
The 2015 Series AB Bonds will matu re on the dates and in the principa l amounts set forth on the inside 
cover page of this Official Statement.  The 2015 Series  AB Bonds will be issued in fully registered form in 
denominations of $5,000 or any integral multiple of $5,000. 
Securities Depository and Book-Entry Only System 
The 2015 Series AB Bonds will be issued in fully registered form, registered in the name of Cede & Co., as 
nominee for The Depository Trust Company (“DTC”), New York, New York, as the Owner of the 2015 Series AB 
Bonds. 
So long as DTC, or its nominee, Cede & Co., is the Owner of the 2015 Series AB Bonds, all payments on 
the 2015 Series AB Bonds will be made directly to DTC.  Disbursement of such payments to the DTC Participants 
will be the responsibility of DTC, and disbursement of such payments to the Beneficial Owners of the 2015 
Series AB Bonds will be the responsibility of the DTC Participants.  See APPENDIX E - SECURITIES 
DEPOSITORY AND THE BOOK-ENTRY SYSTEM. 
Redemption 
Optional Redemption.  The 2015 Series AB Bonds maturing prior to November 1, 2025 are not subject to 
redemption prior to maturity.  The 2015 Series AB Bonds maturing on and after November 1, 2025 are subject to 
redemption prior to their stated maturity, at the option of the SFPUC, from any source of available funds, as a whole 
or in part, on any date on or after May 1, 2025, and if in part by lot within such maturity of 2015 Series A Bonds or 
2015 Series B Bonds, at a redemption price equal to 100% of the principal amount of 2015 Series A Bonds or 2015 
Series B Bonds to be redeemed, plus accrued interest thereon to the date fixed for redemption, without premium. 
Mandatory Sinking Fund Redemption.  The 2015 Series A Bonds maturing on November 1, 2039, and 
November 1, 2045, are further subject to redemption prior to their stated maturity, from amounts on deposit in the 
2015 Series AB Bond Retirement Account, on any November 1 on or after November 1, 2037, and November 1, 
2040, respectively, by lot within any such  maturity if less than all of the 2015 Series A Bonds of such maturity and 
tenor be redeemed, upon payment of the principal amount thereof and accrued interest thereon to the date fixed for 
redemption, without premium.
