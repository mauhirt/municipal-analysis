DESIGNATION AS GREEN BONDS 
The Commission has designated the 2021 Series A Bonds as “Green Bonds” due to the adherence of the projects 
refinanced by the 2021 Series A Bonds with the federal Safe Drinking Water Act and the Clean Water Act. The purpose 
of identifying the 2021 Series A Bonds as Green Bonds is to allow investors to invest directly in bonds that finance 
environmentally beneficial projects in the City.  The Green Bonds are issued on a parity with the 2021 Series B Bonds and 
other Senior Bonds issued by the Commission pursuant to the Resolution.  Holders of the Green Bonds do not assume any 
specific project risk related to any of the projects refunded with the proceeds of the Green Bonds. Descriptions of projects 
refunded with the proceeds of the Green Bonds are included in Appendix H – “Green Bond Project Descriptions.” 
The net proceeds of the Green Bonds are expected to be applied on the closing date to fund an escrow to defease 
the Advance Refunded Bonds.
