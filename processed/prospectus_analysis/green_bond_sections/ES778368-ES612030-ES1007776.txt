Green Bonds Designation.  The City is issuing the Series 2016B Bonds as “Green Bonds” based 
upon the environmental benefits of the capital projects to be financed with proceeds of the Series 2016B 
Bonds.  The City has designated the capital projects to be financed with the Series 2016B Bonds as 
“Green Projects” due to the fact that such projects are designed to be environmentally beneficial in the 
broad category of clean water and sustainable waste management.  The purpose of the “Green Bond" 
d
esignation is to allow holders of the Series 2016B Bonds to invest directly in projects that are beneficial 
to the environment and is not intended to provide or imply that a holder of the Series 2016B Bonds is 
entitled to any additional security.  The City intends to comply with the Green Bond Principles (“GBP”) 
as outlined by the International Capital Market Association (“ICMA”) in effect as of the date of issuance 
of the Series 2016B Bonds, the City does not make any representation that the Series 2016B Bonds will 
comply with any future changes to the GBP enacted after the date of issuance of the Series 2016B Bonds, 
as the GBP may be modified by ICMA in the future. 
 
The City’s Office of Financial Services will track the use of the proceeds of the Series 2016B 
Bonds and the City will post to EMMA an annual update regarding the use of the proceeds of the Series 
2016B Bonds until they are fully expended.  Once all proceeds of the Series 2016B Bonds have been 
expended, the City will not post any further updates to EMMA regarding the expenditure of the Series 
2016B proceeds.
