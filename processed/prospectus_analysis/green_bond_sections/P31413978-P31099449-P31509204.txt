Designation as Green Bonds. The City and the Authority are designating the 2021 Bonds a “Green 
Bonds.”  The projects to be funded with the proceeds of the 2021 Bonds will be used and the bonds to be 
refunded were used, for projects improving sustainable water management and environmentally sustainable 
management of living natural resources and land uses, as is generally designed to comport with the Green 
Bond Principles promulgated by the International Capital Market Association (“ICMA”).  The term “Green 
Bonds” is not defined in the Indenture.  The Owners of the 2021 Bonds do not have any security other than 
as provided in the Indenture and as described herein, nor do the owners of the 2021 Bonds assume any 
specific project risk related to any of the projects funded with the proceeds of the 2021 Bonds.  “Green 
Bonds” are entirely self-designating labels lacking any objective guidelines or criteria.  ICMA is a 
European-based entity with some members from the United States.  The City assumes no obligation to 5 
ensure that the projects financed with the proceeds of the 2021 Bonds comply with any legal or other 
standards or principles that may relate to “Green Bonds.” Additionally, the 2021 Bonds have been 
designated BAM GreenStar Bonds because BAM has determined that the use of bond proceeds by the City 
as described in this Official Statement and in any additional information obtained by BAM aligns with one 
of the Green Bond Principles (“GBPs”) developed by ICMA.
