Green Bond Designation & Green Projects 
The mission of the Division is to provide supe rior leadership, excellent customer service 
and innovative stormwater and wast ewater infrastructure management for a safe and sustainable 
environment. The Division is currently undertak ing a number of capital improvement projects 
relating to the acquisition, construction and operation of Waste Water Facilities designed to fulfill 
its mission. Such facilities include  wastewater collection facilities which meet the discharge 
standards in the National Pollution Discha rge Elimination System (“NPDES”) permit and 
wastewater collection and pumping  facilities which reduce sewage spills. Accordingly, the City 
has designated the capital improvements to be fi nanced with the proceed s of the Series 2016 
Bonds as “Green Projects” based on the enviro nmental benefits of these capital improvements 
and has designated the Series 2016 Bonds, the proceeds of which will be used to pay Capital 
Costs of such capital improvements, as “Green Bonds.”
