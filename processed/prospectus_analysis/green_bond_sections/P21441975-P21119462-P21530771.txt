GREEN BONDS DESIGNATION 
The SFMTA has designated the Series 2021C Bonds as “Green Bonds” based on the intended use of the 
proceeds of the Series 2021C Bonds to finance four  environmental beneficial projects, including (i) the 1200 15th 
Street Renovation project; (ii) the Train Control System Upgrade project; (iii) the Parking Meter Replacement project; 
and (iv) the Transit Optimization project, each as further described under “SERIES 2021C PROJECTS.”  
20 
The SFMTA engaged S&P Global Ratings (“ S&P”) to provide a Green Transaction Evaluation score 
pursuant to S&P’s Green Transaction Evaluation criteria.  Although the SFMTA considers all of the projects listed 
above to be green projects, S&P dee med the Parking Meter Replacement project to be outside the scope of its Green 
Transaction Evaluation framework.  Based on its evaluation of the remaining projects for which 81% of the project 
funds are intended to be used, S&P gave the financing of such projects a Green Transaction Evaluation score of E2/74. 
The SFMTA has determined that the Series 2021C Bonds issued to finance the projects not evaluated by 
S&P will consist of the Series 2021C Bonds in the aggregate principal amount of $19,815,000 maturing March 1, 
2046.  The SFMTA has further determined that the balance of the Series 2021C Bonds in the aggregate principal 
amount of $84,470,000 maturing March 1, 2051 will be issued to finance the projects evaluated by S&P.  
A copy of the Green Transac tion Evaluation report, which describes S&P’s Green Transaction Evaluation 
criteria and provides the rationale for the SFMTA’s Green Transaction Evaluation score, is attached as Appendix H 
to this Official Statement.
